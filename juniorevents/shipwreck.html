About This Event

Imagine a ship is sinking, and there is only one life jacket left. Each participant represents a different animal or species (for example: Honeybee, Coral, or even a Human).

Participants must argue why their chosen species deserves the life jacket the most. The person who proves their species is the most useful or important to the planet gets the life jacket and survives.
