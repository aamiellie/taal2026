About BCM College

Bishop Chulaparambil Memorial College (BCM College), one of the first colleges for women in Kerala, situated at the heart of Kottayam, was founded in 1955 with the objective of empowering women through education. Over the decades, the college has grown into a premier institution known for its academic excellence and holistic development, offering undergraduate and postgraduate programmes across arts, science, and commerce streams. The institution emphasizes value-based education, discipline, and social responsibility, nurturing confident and socially committed graduates through a vibrant campus culture. With a dedicated faculty and strong academic traditions, BCM College continues to inspire generations of learners and contribute meaningfully to society.
